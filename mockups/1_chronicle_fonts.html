In the year of our reckoning, 1085 — the first year of the reign

M ormund, in his 26th year, did sit the throne of Corwick.
— said to be Shrewd, and Patient of temper.

Treasury 200 gold  ·  Levy of arms 300  ·  Souls 5,000  ·  Tax 25%

Prosperity50
