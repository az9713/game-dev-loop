Realm — visual style mockups

Three looks for the same game moment. Open each, then tell me which feels right.

Style 1

Illuminated Chronicle

Warm parchment, red & gold ink, ornate lettering. Your reign reads like an illustrated storybook.
Style 2

Throne Hall

Dark stone, candle-glow, gold accents, your house banner. Moody and kingly.
Style 3

War Table / Map

A parchment map of your realm and neighbours, with a council panel of stats and decrees. Most strategy-game-like.

All three are drawn entirely in code — no images, no fonts to download, nothing to install. This is the actual technique the finished game would use, so what you see is honestly representative of the look you'd get.
